The answers, plainly.

Is SimVida a financial advisor?
No. SimVida is the technology platform your advisor uses to work with you. All advice comes from real, licensed human advisors. Our job is to vet who we let onto the platform, and to build tools that make their work clearer and more transparent.
What does SimVida actually cost me?
Nothing. SimVida is free for you to use. Your advisor pays a seat fee to use the platform, and that's how SimVida is funded. What you invest, how, and where, is a separate conversation between you and your advisor.
What if I don't have an advisor yet?
We'll help you find one. Every advisor on SimVida is vetted for transparency and the kind of service you deserve. You stay in control: try, switch, leave, whenever you want.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Bank-grade encryption, POPIA-compliant. You own your data. We'll never sell it. You can export it or delete it at any time.
Can I leave?
Yes, any time, without penalty. No lock-in. Take your data with you.
Who's behind SimVida?
Eugene Maree, a 25-year veteran of SA financial services, and Peter Koen, a designer with 20 years in the industry. The full team is here.
Is this a robo-advisor?
No. SimVida isn't an advisor at all, robo or otherwise. It's the platform your advisor uses to work with you. Every advisor on SimVida is real, human, and vetted by us before we let them on.
