Questions we get asked at demos.

Does SimVida replace my existing CRM / practice management tool?
For most advisors, yes. SimVida handles client records, document storage, proposals, scenarios, and practice-level reporting in one place. We'll walk you through what to keep and what to move during onboarding.
How long does onboarding take?
A typical practice is up and running in {ONBOARDING_DAYS}. We help import your existing client data, configure branches and roles, and run a team training session.
Where does SimVida sit on compliance?
SimVida is a technology platform, not a financial service provider. You remain the FSCA-licensed entity, and SimVida is the tooling you use to manage your practice and serve your clients. We're POPIA-compliant, use bank-grade encryption, and the platform supports the record-keeping and disclosure workflows you're accountable for. Full technical and data-handling documentation available on request.
Can clients see everything I see?
No. Access is role-based. Clients see their own picture (assets, cover, scenarios you've shared). You see your full practice view, plus per-client analytics. Nothing is shared without your explicit action.
What if I'm not ready to go all-in? Can I start with just part of my book?
Yes. Start with a few clients, prove the value, then roll out the rest. We'll support you through both stages.
What about clients who don't want a human advisor?
Clients come to SimVida because they want guidance from someone they trust. If a client genuinely wants no human involvement, we're probably not their platform, and that's OK. In practice, it's rare: most people want an advisor, they just want one who's on their side.
Do you integrate with LISPs and product providers?
Yes. We integrate with most major South African LISPs and product providers. List of current integrations available at demo. New integrations ship regularly.
