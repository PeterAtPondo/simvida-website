Privacy, in plain English.

This is a plain-language summary of how SimVida handles your data. Last updated {POLICY_DATE}. The full, lawyer-reviewed policy is available on request.

In short

Your data is yours. We don't sell it. We share it only where you've asked us to. You can export or delete it at any time.

The short version.

  • Your data is yours. You can export it or delete it at any time.
  • We don't sell your data. We never will.
  • We share it only where you've asked us to. Your advisor sees what you choose to share.
  • We're POPIA-compliant. Registered with the Information Regulator. Information officer details in our PAIA manual.
  • Bank-grade encryption, at rest and in transit.
  • Servers in South Africa for primary storage. Compliant backups held in EU jurisdictions.

What we collect.

You tell us: name, contact details, financial information you upload, messages you send your advisor.

We observe: how you use the product (for improving it). Device and browser type (for security). IP address (for fraud prevention).

We don't collect: location beyond what your IP implies, biometrics, or anything we don't need.

Who sees what.

You see everything in your account.

Your advisor sees what you've chosen to share with them. You control the granularity and can revoke access at any time.

SimVida staff access client data only when you've raised a support request, or for legal/regulatory reasons. Every access is logged.

Third-party processors (payment providers, infrastructure hosts) see only what's needed for their specific role. Full list available on request.

Your rights under POPIA.

  • Access a copy of your data
  • Correct anything that's wrong
  • Delete your account and data
  • Object to specific processing
  • Complain to the Information Regulator

To exercise any of these: email privacy@simvida.com. We'll respond within 5 working days.

Changes to this policy.

If we change anything material, we'll email you and flag it on sign-in. We won't change anything retroactively in a way that reduces your rights.
