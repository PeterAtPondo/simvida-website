What we've shipped, and what's on the way.

We release small improvements every week. Bigger features drop every couple of months. Here's what's been landing.

April 2026 · Feature

Side-by-side retirement scenarios for advisors.

Build two retirement paths in parallel, tweak the sliders, and see the difference in one view. Shared with your client in one click. Designed for live conversations, not post-meeting proposals.

April 2026 · Improvement

Cleaner client onboarding.

Bulk-import existing client books. Smart field mapping. Practice-level document uploads. Advisors were spending two hours per client; now it's {ONBOARDING_MINUTES}.

March 2026 · Feature

The client dashboard got its heartbeat.

A single "how are you doing" indicator on every client view, backed by six measurable signals (cover gaps, contribution trend, retirement projection, fee efficiency, documentation completeness, recent engagement). Early days, already changing the shape of client conversations.

March 2026 · Improvement

Vetting process for advisors, documented.

We get asked "how do you decide which advisors are on the platform" a lot. So we wrote it down. Published criteria, published review cadence, published removal process.

February 2026 · Feature

Plain-English policy summaries.

Upload any insurance or investment statement; we extract the terms that actually matter (cover amount, beneficiary, escalation, surrender cost) and write them back in words a normal person can read. Still conservative on what we'll auto-extract; we'd rather leave a field blank than lie about it.

Coming soon

Vida, the conversation co-pilot.

An AI layer that helps advisors spot the conversations worth having this week. Cover gaps. Fee reviews. Milestone birthdays. Clients who could use a check-in. In development; shaped by the advisors currently piloting it. Launch window: {VIDA_LAUNCH}.
