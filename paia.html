In short

This page tells you how to ask SimVida for a copy of records we hold about you, under South African law. Our Information Officer is the point of contact; fees apply per the PAIA regulations.

The Information Officer is the point of contact for all requests for access to records held by SimVida.

Records held by SimVida.

SimVida holds the following categories of records:

  • Client and advisor account records (names, contact details, linked practice)
  • Financial information provided by users (assets, policies, documents)
  • Communication records between clients and advisors on the platform
  • Company records (founding documents, financials, tax, employment)
  • Operational records (server logs, audit trails, security incidents)

How to request access.

Use the prescribed Form 2 (available from the Information Regulator), completed and addressed to the Information Officer above.

We respond within 30 days. Fees apply per the PAIA regulations; details available on request.

If we refuse your request, you have the right to internal appeal, and then to apply to the Information Regulator or the courts.

Information automatically available.

The following records are freely available without a formal PAIA request:

  • This PAIA manual
  • Our Privacy policy and Terms of use
  • Marketing and product information on our website
  • Your own personal data (accessible within your account, exportable on request)
